How it works

One client. Eleven rolls. A complete birth story.

You'll name your client, then roll for her health, her baby, her partner, and her provider. From there you'll walk through pregnancy, labor, birth, and postpartum. Use each scenario to practice what you would say and do as her doula.

5

After the postpartum roll, you'll see your full birth story plus reflection prompts to debrief with your peer or coordinator.

How it works

One client. Thirteen rolls. A complete birth story.

You'll name your client, then roll for who she is and who's with her. From there you'll walk through pregnancy, labor, birth, the first weeks, and her 8-week check-in. Along the way you'll face doula decision moments and practice BRAIN responses.

Step Zero

Choose your difficulty.

Difficulty weights how often hard scenarios appear. Cascades are on at every level: rolls influence later rolls so the story stays internally consistent.

After your final roll you'll see a written summary of the birth story, your doula choices, and reflection prompts you can take to debrief.

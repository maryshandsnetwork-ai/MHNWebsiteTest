Our Network

The hands behind every birth.

254 trained volunteer doulas serving families across all five Louisiana healthcare regions.

Our doulas are mothers, nurses, students, ministers, full-time professionals, and full-time community caregivers. They look like the families they serve — a rare concordance in the doula profession and a core part of why our outcomes are what they are.

What Our Doulas Bring

  • ICEA-approved Birth Doula training with ongoing mentorship from senior MHN staff
  • Bereavement training for those serving families experiencing loss
  • Bilingual capacity — many MHN doulas serve in English and Spanish
  • Continuity of care from prenatal visits through postpartum check-ins
  • Cultural humility and lived community membership in the regions they serve

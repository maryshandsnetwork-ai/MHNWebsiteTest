Text a Doula · MHN Hotline

When you need us, day or night.

(225) 424-7532

Free 24/7 support from trained doulas. Quick questions? We're here for you.

Text a Doula is a free service available to everyone — whether you're pregnant, recently gave birth, or just need someone to talk with through this season. This is your direct line to a trained Mary's Hands Network staff doula.

Bilingual support available! We offer support in English and Spanish.

Text your question or concern any time, and get real answers from a real person who cares.

Perfect For

  • Moms due soon who don't have time to go through the full birth intake process
  • Quick questions about pregnancy symptoms, labor signs, or postpartum recovery
  • Help finding resources like WIC, Medicaid, housing, diapers, or mental health support
  • Support during challenging moments — newborn care, breastfeeding, or emotional wellness

How to Use the Text Line

  • Text your question or concern any time
  • A staff doula will respond within 24 hours (usually much sooner during business hours)
  • We can answer questions, help you find resources, and offer follow-up questions or just check in. We're here to support you.
  • You can ask follow-up questions or just check in. We're here to support you.

Response Times

Business Hours (Mon-Fri, 10am-4pm): Usually within 1-2 hours
After Hours & Weekends: Within 24 hours

Text a Doula is NOT for

We're here to support, not replace emergency care or long-term clinical services. Please reach out to the appropriate resources for:

  • Medical emergencies: Call 911 or go to your nearest hospital
  • Medical diagnoses or prescriptions: We can offer support and information, but not medical advice
  • Inappropriate, abusive, or spam messages

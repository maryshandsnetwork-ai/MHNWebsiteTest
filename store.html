Every purchase helps fund free volunteer doula care for Louisiana families. Shipping happens within five business days; orders go through PayPal's secure checkout.

How to wire up these products (remove this block when ready)

  1. Go to paypal.com → Pay & Get Paid → PayPal Buttons and create a "Buy Now" hosted button for each product. Set price, name, and shipping options there.
  2. After saving each button, PayPal gives you a hosted_button_id. Copy it.
  3. In store.html, replace the placeholder #TODO_BUY_BUTTON_ID in each <a class="buy"> with https://www.paypal.com/cgi-bin/webscr?cmd=_s-xclick&hosted_button_id=YOUR_ID.
  4. Update each product's name, price, description, and photo (drop a JPG into assets/photos/ and update the <img src>).
  5. Once everything's plugged in and tested, delete this whole "setup-note" block.

Volunteer with Mary's Hands Network.

One mission. Many ways to serve.

Whether you are a trained doula, a new student, or someone who simply wants to support families in your community, there is a place for you at Mary's Hands Network. Our volunteers make free, equitable, high-quality perinatal support possible throughout Louisiana.

Every role helps. Every hour matters. Every person belongs.

Who Can Volunteer?

We welcome individuals who are:

  • Passionate about maternal and infant health, equity, and community care
  • 18 years or older (some roles open to high school volunteers)
  • Able to commit to training, scheduling, and showing up consistently
  • Doulas (trained or in training) ready to serve free of charge
  • Professionals with skills to share (administrative, media, interpreting, and more)

No medical or nursing experience is required to help.

Explore Your Options, Find Your Voice

Building Your Birth Vision is a simple guide to help you explore your options, share your hopes, and start meaningful conversations with your care team.

Think of it as a map for your birth journey, not a rigid plan, but a tool to prepare while staying flexible when things change. Use it to reflect, ask questions, and make informed decisions with confidence as you welcome your baby into the world.

What's Inside the Guide

  • Reflection prompts to clarify your values, hopes, and fears
  • Questions to bring to your provider visits
  • Comfort measures and labor preferences worksheet
  • Postpartum and newborn care planning
  • Space to capture your support team and backup plans

How to Use It

Print it, fill it out at your own pace, and bring it to your prenatal visits. Share it with your partner, your doula, and your provider. Update it as your pregnancy progresses — your vision can evolve too.

"Every birth deserves a vision shaped by the woman living it."

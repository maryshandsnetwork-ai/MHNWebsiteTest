Our Mission

We empower families, promote birth excellence, and bridge the health equity gap in Louisiana.

Louisiana consistently ranks among the worst states for maternal health outcomes. Our maternal mortality rate stands at 58.1 per 100,000 live births, more than twice the national average. Black women face a mortality risk three to four times higher than white women.

This is the gap MHN was built to address. Through trained volunteer doulas, wrap-around community services, and IRB-approved outcomes data, we bring evidence-based, culturally humble care to every family who applies, regardless of income, background, or zip code.
